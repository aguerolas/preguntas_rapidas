Risk 1: Credential Exposure Through Manual Entry
Each WinSCP session requires manual input of credentials, increasing the likelihood of exposure through shoulder surfing, keystroke logging, or inadvertent storage in unsecured locations.
Risk 2: Unauthorized or Erroneous File Transmission
The manual nature of the file transfer process introduces the possibility of sending incorrect or unauthorized confidential information to the BCRP, whether due to human error or lack of a validation control prior to submission.

The manual nature of the file transfer process introduces the possibility of retrieving or delivering incorrect or unauthorized confidential cardholder information, whether due to human error or lack of validation control prior to processing. The absence of automated verification and centralized logging exposes the institution to potential breaches of data confidentiality and integrity, and limits non-repudiation of the transfer activity.


The absence of an automated file transfer mechanism and centralized audit trail for WinSCP sessions introduces the risk of wrong or unauthorized file handling. Without a validation control or audit trail, the organization cannot ensure data integrity or non-repudiation of the embossed card files exchanged with the vendors Prosegur and DocFlow.
